Passion Fruit Mousse

Description

Brazil’s passion fruit mousse, or mousse de maracujá, is a dessert that effortlessly steals the spotlight. The vibrant, tangy juice of maracujá delivers a tart flavor that’s as bold as it is refreshing. It’s a treat that makes you wonder why you ever settled for bland pudding.

Ingredients

  • 1 can (14 oz or 397 g) of sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 can (7.6 oz or 225 g) of Brazilian table cream (such as Nestlé Media Crema)
  • 200 ml (about 7 oz) of concentrated passion fruit juice (100% juice, store-bought)
  • Pulp of 1 passion fruit (about 1-2 tablespoons, optional)

Steps

  1. Pour one 14 oz (395 g) can of sweetened condensed milk into a blender.
  2. Add 200 ml of concentrated passion fruit juice to the blender.
  3. Add one 7.6 oz (225 g) can of table cream (such as Nestlé Media Crema) to the blender.
  4. Blend for 5 seconds, then stir the mixture with a spoon to ensure an even consistency.
  5. Pour the mixture into five 4-ounce bowls (yields about 5 portions) or one large bowl, if preferred.
  6. Refrigerate for at least 3 hours to set.
  7. Optional: Garnish with a small spoonful of fresh passion fruit pulp before serving.
